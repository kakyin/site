How to Buy?

Step 1

Add Metamask extension to your browser and fund it with some BNB coins.

Step 2

Go to pancackeswap.finance and set the trading pair BNB-CPBC.

Step 3

Now you can swap your BNB for CPBC. Set your slippage to 5-6% . Click on the Swap Button to finalize your trade.
